About Perodua

Perodua is a Malaysian automotive company that specializes in the production of compact cars. Established in 1993, the company has become one of the most popular and trusted automobile manufacturers in Malaysia and Southeast Asia.


Perodua is known for producing affordable, reliable, and fuel-efficient vehicles that cater to the needs of a diverse range of customers. The company's product lineup includes the Axia, Bezza, Myvi, Alza, and Aruz, which are all designed to meet the specific requirements of Malaysian and international markets.


With over 25 years of experience in the automotive industry, Perodua has become a trusted brand that is synonymous with quality, reliability, and affordability. The company's commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction has earned it a reputation as one of the most respected and successful automobile manufacturers in Malaysia and Southeast Asia.
